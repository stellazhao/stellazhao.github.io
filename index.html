Work in New York City. Dream beyound the world.

I was born and raised in an engineering family in China. Since I was little, my mother had alaways told me, "When you grow up, you are going to see the world." At the age of 16, I took my first trip to the US for a summer camp. When I first stood in Wall Street, thinking only the most intelligent and diligent people could make it here, New York City became a part of my dream. At 18, I packed two suit cases, travelled alone across the pacific ocean and started college in Seattle, and then graduate school in Ithaca. Half the globe and 7 years later, I now work in NYC and live my teenage dream. But, the journey would not just stop here. I miss the crisp air and evergreen plants in Pacific North West; I heard about the beautiful California sunshine; I've tasted delicious German sausages; and I dream to call a Caribbean island my home some day. Life is an adventure. Dream big. Work hard. The world is my oyster.
